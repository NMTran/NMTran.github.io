Our Research
Vision Science

We use the retina as an approachable model to study the nervous system.

Neural Repair

We are particularly interested in studying the mechanisms by which the nervous system can repair itself after injury.

Molecular Genetics

We use molecular genetics techniques to gain insight into retina biology in both mice and stem cells.

Single-cell Genomics

We apply functional genomic techniques and data analysis to examine retina biology and neuroregeneration at scale.
